It is about hello kitty!

Hello Kitty is a fictional character produced by the Japanese company Sanrio. Created by Yuko Shimizu, Hello Kitty first appeared on a vinyl coin purse in 1974 and quickly became a global phenomenon. The character is depicted as a cute anthropomorphic white cat with a red bow and no visible mouth. Hello Kitty has since expanded into various merchandise, including toys, clothing, accessories, and even themed cafes. The character's popularity has made it an iconic symbol of Japanese pop culture and a beloved figure worldwide.
